Resume Writing Guide

Follow these expert tips to build a strong and professional resume that stands out.

1. Keep It Concise

Recruiters typically spend only 6-10 seconds on a resume. Keep it to one page and include only the most relevant details.

2. Use a Professional Format

Ensure your resume is clean, well-structured, and easy to read. Use consistent fonts and spacing.

3. Highlight Your Achievements

Use action words and quantify your accomplishments. Example: "Increased sales by 30% in six months."

4. Tailor It to Each Job

Customize your resume for each job application by focusing on the skills and experience relevant to the job.
